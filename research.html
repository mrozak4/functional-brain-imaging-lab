Research Areas

Pillar 01

Neurovascular Coupling

We investigate the mechanisms underlying neurovascular coupling, the process by which neural activity is linked to changes in cerebral blood flow. Understanding this relationship is crucial for interpreting fMRI signals and identifying neurovascular impairments in diseases like Alzheimer's and stroke.

Pillar 02

Disease Models

Our research applies these advanced imaging techniques to preclinical models of Alzheimer's Disease, Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI), and stroke, uncovering early metabolic and vascular changes to discover new therapeutic targets.

Pillar 03

Deep Learning & Pipeline Automation

We develop automated, label-efficient deep learning pipelines to reconstruct microvascular networks from microscopy data, accelerating the analysis of massive multi-photon imaging datasets.
