Our Facilities

Facility 01

Two-Photon Fluorescence Microscopy

Our custom-built microscopy setup enables high-resolution 3D imaging of cerebral microcirculation in vivo. Equipped for simultaneous multi-modal recordings, optogenetic stimulation, and advanced fluorescence detection.

Facility 02

Preclinical MRI

State-of-the-art preclinical MRI capabilities for assessing functional connectivity, structural changes, and metabolic alterations in rodent models of neurological diseases.
