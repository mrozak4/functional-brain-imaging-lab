Life in Toronto

Toronto is a vibrant, diverse city with a thriving scientific community. As a hub for both neuroscience research and artificial intelligence, it offers an incredible environment for interdisciplinary collaboration.

Our lab is located at the Sunnybrook Research Institute, offering state-of-the-art facilities surrounded by green space, with easy transit access to downtown Toronto.
