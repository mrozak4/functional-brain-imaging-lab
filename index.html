Sunnybrook Research Institute

Mapping the Mind in Motion

Welcome to the Functional Brain Imaging Lab (fBIL) at the Sunnybrook Research Institute! We are keen on developing tools for studying the inner workings of the coolest organ of the body and examining new ways of supporting recovery processes in Alzheimer’s disease, stroke, and traumatic brain injury.

Our Research Pillars

Neurovascular Coupling

Investigating how blood flow dynamically adjusts to neuronal activity in health and neurodegenerative diseases.

Two-Photon Microscopy

High-resolution 3D imaging of cerebral microcirculation and neuronal population dynamics.

Functional MRI

Non-invasive brain function imaging at high field strengths to connect cellular mechanisms to macroscopic signals.

Optogenetics

Precise light-activated neuronal stimulation combined with multi-modal recording techniques.
